"The Aroma of Tacoma"

  • The "Aroma of Tacoma", also known as the Tacoma Aroma, is a putrid and unpleasant odor associated with Tacoma, Washington.

  • Tacoma is located in the western part of the state, in Pierce County.

  • The smell has been described as similar to the odor of rotten eggs.

  • The odor is not noticeable throughout the city, but is rather concentrated in the Tacoma Tideflats and is frequently smelled by motorists traveling that section of Interstate 5.

Pizza Philosophy
Food for All Nostrils

Food authenticity is a huge deal with us. From each source it comes from, we built a system that allows for each individual testing of food units with integration tests of food quality control. We work with our business partners to ensure maximum smell and taste is deployed straight into our restaurant production environment. If it doesn't pass our internal testing, we won't serve it to you. It's that simple.
