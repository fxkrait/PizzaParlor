"The Aroma of Tacoma"

  • The "Aroma of Tacoma", also known as the Tacoma Aroma, is a putrid and unpleasant odor associated with Tacoma, Washington.

  • Tacoma is located in the western part of the state, in Pierce County.

  • The smell has been described as similar to the odor of rotten eggs.

  • The odor is not noticeable throughout the city, but is rather concentrated in the Tacoma Tideflats and is frequently smelled by motorists traveling that section of Interstate 5.
